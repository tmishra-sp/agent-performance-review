# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

Expected behavior:
- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus critique on ideas and implementation details.
- Provide actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent, clarify before escalating.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private/sensitive information.
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content that violates this Code of Conduct.
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to project maintainers privately through repository security/contact channels.
